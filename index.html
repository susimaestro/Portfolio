Tiffin Café Website

This week I’m taking you on the journey from start to finished high fidelity prototype with animations for Mobile and Desktop. The client is a local coffee shop specializing in vegetarian cuisine. Starting with user and business research, implementing that data into a concept and finally translating that into a prototype. The goal is to make a product that the café owners would be proud of and that would result in more visibility and traffic. Ready to rumble?

Microsite for London Coffee Festival

For the 6th challenge at the UX/UI Ironhack Bootcamp, we had to create a microsite for an event. I was teamed up with Ines and Gui and we decided to work on a microsite for ‘The London Coffee Festival’, a fun vibrant festival that celebrates coffee culture. It’s aimed at industry professionals as well as coffee and food lovers. The team started creating the 2020 edition both on Mobile and Desktop.
